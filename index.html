Act (A Conference Toolkit) is a multilingual, template-driven, multi-conference web site that can manage the users, talks, schedule and payment for your conference.

[% WRAPPER box %]

Documentation

You can have a look at the available Act documentation (useful for conference organizers using Act).

[% END %] [% WRAPPER box %]

Mailing-lists

There are three mailing-lists for Act, for:

  • Act users (this is the place where you can send bug reports, complaints, praise for the software, requests for help setting up your Act system or for getting your conference hosted by mongueurs.net),
  • Act developers (this is where developers hang out, talking about developers' stuff),

[% END %] [% WRAPPER box %]
